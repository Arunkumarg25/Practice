Columns used by each test case

register | subregister | approve | login | changefp | sendfeedback | addcustomers | editcustomers | delcustomers
addcontacts | addrules | editcontacts | editrules | delcontacts | delrules | addreports | delschedulefrequency | delallschedule

register - registers agents

subregister - registers subagents

approve - approves agents or subagents

login - logs in as agent or subagent and accept terms & conditions if apply

changefp - (admin only) changes the feature pack of customers one by one

sendfeedback - sends feedback on every page

addcustomers - adds customers as agent or subagent

editcustomers - edits existing customers as agent or subagent

delcustomers - deletes customers as agent or subagent

addcontacts - adds delivery contacts as agent or subagent

addrules - adds delivery rules as agent or subagent

editcontacts - edits delivery contacts as agent or subagent

editrules - edits delivery rules as agent or subagent

delcontacts - deletes delivery contacts for customers as agent or subagent

delrules - deletes delivery rules for customers as agent or subagent

addreports - adds one time or scheduled reports as agent or subagent

delschedulefrequency - deletes scheduled reports based on the frequency of the reports

delallschedule - deletes all scheduled reports
